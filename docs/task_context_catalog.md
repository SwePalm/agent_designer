# Task & Context Catalog

## Task focus options

1. **Meeting notes summarizer**
   *Goal:* Turn raw meeting transcripts into concise summaries with decisions and action items.
2. **Email triage helper**
   *Goal:* Classify inbox messages by urgency, topic, and owner; propose draft replies.
3. **Product release brief generator**
   *Goal:* Draft a release summary tailored for internal or customer-facing audiences.
4. **Customer feedback synthesizer**
   *Goal:* Aggregate feedback themes, sentiment, and top requests across channels.
5. **Incident postmortem drafter**
   *Goal:* Structure timelines, root-cause analysis, and follow-up actions from incident data.
6. **Sales call recap builder**
   *Goal:* Extract key needs, objections, and next steps from call notes.
7. **Research digest composer**
   *Goal:* Summarize research sources into a brief with citations and open questions.
8. **Project status updater**
   *Goal:* Roll up progress, blockers, and risks for weekly status reporting.

## Context types

1. **Company glossary**
   *Description:* Acronyms, product names, internal jargon, and preferred terminology.
   *Constraints:* Required for all tasks that generate written output to maintain consistent language.
2. **Team process**
   *Description:* Rituals, roles, decision-making steps, and escalation paths.
   *Constraints:* Required for incident postmortem drafter and project status updater.
3. **Product overview**
   *Description:* Product positioning, target personas, and feature boundaries.
   *Constraints:* Required for product release brief generator and customer feedback synthesizer.
4. **Audience profile**
   *Description:* Intended readers, tone, and communication preferences.
   *Constraints:* Required for meeting notes summarizer, sales call recap builder, and research digest composer.
5. **Compliance & privacy rules**
   *Description:* Redaction policies, regulated data, retention requirements.
   *Constraints:* Required for email triage helper and incident postmortem drafter.
6. **Data source inventory**
   *Description:* Approved tools, systems of record, and data freshness.
   *Constraints:* Required for customer feedback synthesizer and project status updater.
7. **Quality bar & success criteria**
   *Description:* Output standards, acceptance thresholds, and review steps.
   *Constraints:* Required for all tasks to define “done.”
8. **Stakeholder map**
   *Description:* Owners, approvers, and escalation contacts.
   *Constraints:* Required for sales call recap builder and incident postmortem drafter.
